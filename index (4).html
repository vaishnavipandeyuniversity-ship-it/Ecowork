✨ The Spirit of Service

Volunteering is more than giving time — it is an act of love for the place you call home. Every hour you spend helping a neighbour, cleaning a street, or guiding a family in need echoes far beyond what the eye can see.

The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.

— Mahatma Gandhi

Volunteers don't necessarily have the time; they just have the heart.

— Elizabeth Andrew

No one has ever become poor by giving. Every act of service plants a seed of hope.

— Anne Frank

In India alone, millions of unsung heroes step forward every day — from cleaning river ghats to teaching underprivileged children, from planting trees to assisting flood victims. Ecowork exists to channel this boundless energy where it matters most. When you register as a volunteer, you become a part of something permanent. Your name, your work, your dedication — recorded and recognised forever in your community's story.
